about me

I am a Masters student in Statistics at LMU Munich. Besides studying I'm working at our department's working group Computational Statistics lead by Prof. Bernd Bischl. My job mainly consists of organizing the course in Computational Statistics. I'm responsible for improving as well as creating lecture slides and holding the tutorial each week to recap the lecture and solve mathematical and programmatical exercises with our students. Besides teaching, the other part of my job is to contribute to the R-package mlr (see Projects), created by my Professor Bernd Bischl. Besides loving my profession I also like travelling and the art of photography, well when I find the time.
